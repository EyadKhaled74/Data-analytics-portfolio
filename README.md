# Superstore Analysis Dashboard

This repository contains an advanced Power BI dashboard project for a Sample Superstore dataset, created during a Data Analyst internship at Uneeq Interns.

## Project Summary

The dashboard evaluates sales performance, profitability, shipping behavior, and regional trends across categories and sub-categories.

## Dashboard Pages

1. **Command Center**
   - Executive KPI summary for Total Sales, Total Profit, Profit Margin, and Average Discount.
   - Regional profit waterfall and segment distribution.

2. **Profit Analytics**
   - Sub-category profit and discount analysis.
   - Cumulative profit percentage and profitability status mapping.
   - Scatter analysis of sales vs profit margin.

3. **Market Mapping**
   - Geographic profit and margin mapping across US states and cities.
   - Regional filter and city-level performance view.

4. **Insights & Operations**
   - Business vs. personal sales segmentation.
   - Operational metrics and performance influencers.
   - Key driver analysis for profitability status.

## Key Insights

- Total Revenue: 2.30M
- Net Profit: 286K
- Avg Profit Margin: 12.01%
- Top sub-categories: Copiers, Phones, Accessories
- Negative profitability found in Tables
- Strongest profit margin in West region
- Central region underperformed
- Standard shipping is dominant; express shipping is underutilized

## Recommendations

- Optimize pricing and discount strategy for low-profit categories such as Tables.
- Focus regional improvements in the Central region.
- Promote high-margin product categories and improve shipping mix for better operational efficiency.
